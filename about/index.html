About

A man documenting life while living it.

Ahmad Memos is the personal platform of Ahmad Asseiri — a place for films, travels, recovery, music, work, and the conversations in between.

This is not a polished persona. It is an archive of lived moments: the beautiful, the confusing, the quiet, and the difficult.

The direction is simple: share what was lived, not what was manufactured. Build films and stories that feel human before they feel perfect.
